My Craft

I woke up in a land so bizzare.
Laying next to a hole shaped like a scar.
A ravine it was, so deep so extended.
But wait, the land, it looks so pixelated.

Everything is shaped like lego blocks.
Trees, hills, rivers, pigs, even a fox.
People! I thought, but they were different.
I can feel that they weren't as intelligent.

I explored and was amazed.
This land is beautiful even if it is strange.
It feels like a game, I'M STUCK IN A GAME!
What even is my name?!

Suddenly a tree was glitching.
Red, Green, Blue, over and over, flashing.
I woke up, it was a dream, I remember my name.
I look outside, and a tree was doing the same.
